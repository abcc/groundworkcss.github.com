Tooltips

Responsive tooltips with smart positioning, resizing with window resize and smart repositioning on window scroll.

Tooltips

Tooltips can be attached to any element by adding class .tooltip and adding a title attribute. When you hover over or focus on the element, the tooltip is displayed.

Tooltips are useful for displaying additional instruction on form elements, describing the behavior of a link or button and much more.

Example Usage:
<input class="tooltip" title="Enter your first & last name" placeholder="Name" />

Example Usage:
<a href="#" class="tooltip" title="This tooltip comes from the `title` attribute of this link">Show me a tooltip!</a>
